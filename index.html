Who are we?

The COVID-19 pandemic of 2020 has had dramatic consequences for populations worldwide. We are students of the Saxion University of Applied Sciences and have been tasked to research the efficacy of DIY mouth masks as well as attempt to come up with reasonably safe designs that can be made at home by people with easily available materials.
